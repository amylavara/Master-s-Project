India Out Of School Children, 2011

The Census 2011 data has revealed an alarming fact that more than 110 million children, of ages 5 to 19 don't go to school at all.

India has 35 states and 640 districts

Total of 110,349,514 children of ages 5 to 19 not attending any educational institution

29.03% children of ages 5 to 19 not attending any educational institution
